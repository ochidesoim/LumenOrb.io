What's the Big Idea, Anyway?

Project LEO is our grand plan to build a sustainable commercial hub in space. We're mixing high-tech engineering with cosmic harmony to redefine orbital manufacturing, research, and logistics.

Ratha Launch System

The celestial chariot. Our reusable launch vehicle designed for reliable and cost-effective access to orbit from Earth.

Vahana Initiative

Solving the 'last-mile' logistics problem with autonomous ferries for cargo and personnel transfer.

Surya Power Array

Solar panels arranged like a lotus flower, capturing the sun's energy to power our ambitions and keep the lights on.

Kavacha Shielding

A multi-layered defense blending advanced composites and a hydrogen-rich gel core to guard the crew against solar flares and cosmic rays.

Varuna Ion Drives

Our hyper-efficient thrusters sip propellant, allowing the LumenOrb to stay perfectly in its orbit for decades with minimal fuss.

Vishwakarma Modules

The commercial heart of the ship. These hot-swappable bays can be a factory, a science lab, or a data center in the sky.

Chakravyu Initiative

Our orbital janitor program, designed to navigate the complex maze of space debris and ensure safe passage for all.

Trials of the Celestial River

An interactive trial in the style of ancient pharaohs to solve the ten great curses of Low Earth Orbit.

Dharma Protocol

The ethical and operational framework ensuring fair, safe, and sustainable practices for all inhabitants and partners of the orbital hub.

Kubera's Orbit

The economic engine. This business model leverages our modular design to generate revenue and foster a self-sustaining commercial ecosystem.
